# Privacy Policy for Google Drive Tree Expander

**Last updated:** February 14, 2026

## Overview

Google Drive Tree Expander is a browser extension that automatically expands the Google Drive sidebar tree to match the current folder path. This extension is developed by an individual developer based in Spain.

## Data Collection

This extension does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user information. No analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind is used.

## Local Storage

The extension stores a single user preference (enabled/disabled toggle) locally on your device using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API. This data never leaves your browser.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services.

## Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out by opening an issue on the extension's support page.
